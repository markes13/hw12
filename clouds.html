Latitude vs. Cloud Cover


When you think of the tropics, do you think of cloudy skies? If you do, you'd probably be disappointed. Generally speaking, the equator is less cloudy than the poles (just compare Hawaii to Alaska). Still, cloud cover is extremely variable, and in the case of this graph, does not seem overly dependent on latitude.
